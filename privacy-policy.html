Privacy Policy

STARTGOLD CAPITAL SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED

This Privacy Policy explains how STARTGOLD CAPITAL SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED ("STARTGOLD", "Company", "We", "Us", "Our", or "Platform") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application, website, and related products and services. This policy is drafted in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Google Play policies.

By accessing or using our Platform, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal data processed in digital form through STARTGOLD's products, services, and platforms. It governs the processing of data of all users, including registered members, visitors, and prospective users.

2. Role of the Company

STARTGOLD acts as a Data Fiduciary under the DPDP Act, 2023. We determine the purpose and means of processing your personal data. Where applicable, we may engage trusted third-party Data Processors (such as KYC partners, payment gateways, and cloud service providers) to process data on our behalf.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

a. Personal Identifiers

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Residential Address

b. Legal and Financial Information

  • PAN (Permanent Account Number)
  • Aadhaar Number (for KYC verification)
  • Bank Account Details
  • UPI ID
  • Transaction History
  • Investment Records

c. Device and Usage Information

  • IP Address
  • Device Type, OS, and Browser Type
  • App Version
  • Click-stream Data and In-app Behavior
  • Crash Logs and Performance Data

d. Location Data

  • Precise and/or approximate location (collected only with explicit user consent)

e. Communication Data

  • Messages sent through in-app support
  • Feedback and reviews
  • Emails or calls to support

f. KYC-Related Data

  • Photographs and selfies
  • Video KYC recordings
  • Document images (PAN card, Aadhaar, etc.)

g. SMS and Contacts

  • SMS access (with explicit consent) for OTP auto-read
  • Contact list access (with explicit consent) for referral features

h. App Metadata

  • Installed app metadata and session data used for fraud detection and security purposes

4. Purpose of Processing

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To create and manage your account, enable gold buying/selling, SIP, staking, and related services
  • Compliance: To comply with applicable laws including AML/KYC regulations and DPDP Act requirements
  • Protection & Security: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other illegal activities
  • Communication: To send transactional updates, promotional offers (with consent), and service-related notifications
  • Product Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, improve user experience, and develop new features
  • Personalization: To tailor content and recommendations based on your preferences and activity

5. Consent and Withdrawal

We process your personal data based on your informed consent, which you provide at the time of registration, KYC, or when accessing specific features. You may withdraw your consent at any time through app settings or by contacting our support team. Please note that withdrawal of consent may limit your access to certain features or services.

6. Data Sharing

We may share your personal data with:

  • Service Providers: Payment gateways, cloud hosting, KYC verification partners, analytics providers
  • Business Partners: Gold supply partners (e.g., MMTC-PAMP, Augmont) and banking/financial partners
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or regulatory direction
  • Corporate Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring

STARTGOLD does not sell your personal data to any third party.

7. Data Storage & Cross-Border Transfers

Your data may be stored on servers located in India or in other countries where our service providers operate. In all cases, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with the DPDP Act.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Role-based access control for internal systems

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. After account deletion or deactivation, we may retain certain data for up to 10 years to comply with legal obligations, including Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations, tax laws, and other statutory requirements.

10. Your Rights

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request information about the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction & Erasure: Request correction of inaccurate data or erasure of data no longer necessary
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: File complaints regarding data processing
  • Right to Nomination: Nominate an individual to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time
