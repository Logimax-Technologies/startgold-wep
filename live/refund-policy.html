Refund Policy

STARTGOLD CAPITAL SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED

Refund, Cancellation and Delivery Return Policy

Effective Date: 06 April 2026

This Refund, Cancellation and Delivery Return Policy forms part of the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy applicable to the StartGOLD platform and shall apply to all users who purchase, sell, redeem, or request physical delivery of digital gold through the StartGOLD platform.

1. Scope

This policy explains the rules governing cancellation, refunds, return-related requests, failed delivery scenarios, and customer obligations in relation to transactions carried out on the StartGOLD platform.

2. No Cancellation After Confirmation

Once a customer confirms an order for purchase of gold, sale of gold, redemption, or physical delivery request, the order becomes final and binding and cannot be cancelled by the customer. Similar digital gold platform terms commonly provide that, once purchase, redemption, or sale-back is confirmed, the request is binding and cannot be cancelled.

3. No Refund for Confirmed Transactions

Amounts paid toward a successfully confirmed purchase order, sale order, redemption order, or delivery request are non-refundable except where a refund is required due to a failed, rejected, duplicate, unauthorized, or technically unsuccessful transaction that is validated by StartGOLD. Industry digital gold terms also distinguish between confirmed orders, which are generally final, and rejected orders, where payment may be returned to the linked bank account.

4. Refunds for Rejected or Failed Transactions

If a transaction is not accepted, fails due to a technical issue, is not completed, or is reversed in accordance with applicable platform controls, StartGOLD may process the applicable refund to the customer’s verified bank account, source account, or original payment mode, subject to internal verification and applicable law. Comparable digital gold terms provide that rejected orders may be refunded and that transaction reversals may result in corresponding credit or debit adjustments.

5. Delivery Address and Verification

Physical delivery of gold products shall be made only to the customer’s registered or delivery address as accepted on the StartGOLD platform and only within serviceable locations. Customers must keep valid proof of identity and any OTP or verification credentials ready at the time of delivery, because digital gold redemption terms commonly require address validation and delivery verification before handover.

6. Failed Delivery Attempts

If no authorized recipient is available at the delivery address, or delivery cannot be completed due to customer-side reasons, StartGOLD or its logistics partner may re-attempt delivery in line with operational practice. Comparable platform terms state that logistics partners may make multiple delivery attempts and, where the customer is unavailable, the item may be returned and re-delivery charges may apply.

7. Return to Vault After Non-Delivery

Where physical delivery cannot be completed after the permitted delivery attempts due to reasons attributable to the customer, the redeemed product may be returned to the designated vault, inventory location, or authorized custodian, subject to StartGOLD’s verification process. In such cases, the customer’s StartGOLD account may be re-credited with the corresponding gold quantity or such adjusted quantity as applicable after verification, while shipping, making, logistics, storage, or other applicable charges may remain payable by the customer; comparable terms provide for re-credit of gold on return of undelivered items and allow non-refund of making or delivery charges where failure is attributable to the customer.

8. Re-Delivery Charges

If a customer requests fresh delivery after a prior failed delivery attributable to the customer, the customer shall be required to pay the applicable shipping, handling, insurance, minting, making, taxes, and other related charges again before re-dispatch. Digital gold delivery terms commonly provide that re-delivery costs may be charged again when an item is returned after unsuccessful delivery attempts.

9. Customer Inspection at Delivery

Customers must inspect the package at the time of receipt and should accept delivery only if the outer package appears untampered and in acceptable condition. Comparable digital gold delivery terms instruct customers not to accept tampered packages and to immediately report such cases for investigation or redelivery review.

10. No Return or Refund After Accepted Delivery

Once the customer or an authorized recipient accepts delivery, confirms receipt, signs the proof of delivery, shares OTP, or otherwise completes delivery acknowledgement, the order shall be treated as fulfilled and no return, cancellation, replacement, or refund shall be permitted except where required under applicable law or where StartGOLD independently verifies fraud, transit tampering, wrong item delivery, or a material defect attributable to StartGOLD or its authorized partners. Comparable digital gold terms state that after delivery acknowledgement, the provider is generally not liable for refund or replacement for later complaints, while Indian consumer protection principles still require fair treatment where goods are defective or not as described.

11. Tampered, Damaged, Wrong or Quality-Related Issues

If a customer receives a package that appears tampered, damaged, defective, or different from the confirmed order, the customer must notify StartGOLD without undue delay, and preferably on the same day of delivery, with clear photographs, unboxing evidence, invoice details, and any other information sought by StartGOLD.

12. Investigation and Resolution

Upon receiving a valid complaint, StartGOLD may investigate the issue directly or through its logistics, minting, vaulting, or platform partners. Based on the findings, StartGOLD may, at its sole but reasonable discretion and subject to applicable law, offer one of the following remedies: re-delivery, replacement, re-credit of gold quantity, refund of applicable charges, refund of transaction amount, or any other fair corrective action appropriate to the circumstances.

13. Refund Timeline

Where a refund is approved by StartGOLD for a valid reason, the refunded amount shall ordinarily be credited to the customer’s verified bank account or original payment source within 5 to 7 working days from the date of approval, subject to banking partner timelines, payment gateway processing cycles, and completion of all required checks. Industry commentary on Indian e-commerce refunds notes that refunds are generally expected within a reasonable period and commonly within about 7 to 14 days, depending on payment flows.

14. Non-Refundable Charges

Unless otherwise expressly approved by StartGOLD or required by law, convenience fees, platform fees, taxes, making charges, delivery charges, insurance charges, payment gateway charges, and other service charges are non-refundable once the related service has been validly rendered or the relevant request has been confirmed. Comparable digital gold terms state that fees and charges, once paid, are non-refundable, and that making and delivery charges may not be refunded where non-delivery is attributable to the customer.

15. Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for providing accurate name, mobile number, address, KYC information, bank account details, and delivery information, and for being available to receive delivery when scheduled. StartGOLD shall not be responsible for refund delays, failed deliveries, or credit issues caused by incorrect information provided by the customer, a position that is also reflected in digital gold platform terms relating to KYC, address, and bank details.

16. Exceptions and Legal Compliance

Nothing in this policy limits any non-excludable rights available to customers under applicable law, including rights arising from proven deficiency in service, defective goods, or delivery of goods materially different from the confirmed order. Indian consumer protection commentary emphasizes that blanket refusal to address defective or wrong goods can amount to unfair practice, even where a general no-refund rule exists for change-of-mind cancellations.

17. Contact for Support

For refund-related assistance, complaint registration, or delivery issue escalation, customers may contact StartGOLD support at support@startgold.com. Customers should include their registered mobile number, transaction reference, order details, and issue description to enable faster resolution.
