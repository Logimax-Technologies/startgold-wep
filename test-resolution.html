⚠️ How to Test 4K on Your Laptop

  1. Press F12 to open DevTools
  2. Click the 📱 phone icon (top-left of DevTools)
  3. Click ⋮ three dots in responsive toolbar → Enable "Add device pixel ratio"
  4. Set DPR dropdown → 1.0
  5. Type 3840 in width field → press Enter
  6. Press Ctrl+Shift+R to hard refresh
